**Hello, I'm David Nuñez.** I explore the messy, marvelous space where humans and machines intertwine.

My work blends research, storytelling, and speculative prototyping to untangle the human threads woven throughout technology that shape our culture, our choices, and our sense of self.

#### More

I’m the Director of Metadata and Knowledge Systems at Harvard Business School’s Baker Library, formerly the founding Director of Technology and Digital Strategy at the MIT Museum, with grad school work at the MIT Media Lab exploring how humans and robots live together.
